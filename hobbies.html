Hobbies & Interests

Beyond academics, I enjoy pursuing various hobbies that help me relax, learn new skills, and express my creativity. Here are some of the activities I'm passionate about during my free time.

Coding Projects

I enjoy working on small coding projects in my free time. From building simple games to creating utility applications, coding allows me to express my creativity while improving my technical skills.

Gaming

Video games provide both entertainment and a way to connect with friends. I particularly enjoy strategy and adventure games that challenge my problem-solving abilities.

Reading

I love immersing myself in books about technology, science fiction, and personal development. Reading broadens my perspective and introduces me to new ideas and concepts.

Photography

Capturing moments through photography allows me to see the world from different perspectives. I particularly enjoy nature and urban landscape photography.

Favorite Activities

In addition to my main hobbies, I also enjoy attending tech meetups, participating in hackathons, and exploring new coffee shops while working on projects. I find that a balance of activities helps keep me motivated and inspired across all areas of my life.
